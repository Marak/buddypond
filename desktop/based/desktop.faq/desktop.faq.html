What is Buddy Pond?


Buddy Pond is an Open-Source Cloud Desktop and Peer to Peer Instant Messenger.


What can Buddy Pond Do?

Buddy Pond can basically do anything a modern Desktop Operating System can do. Try clicking around the Desktop to discover new functionality. Invite another Buddy to join and try out the Remix Multimedia Chats.


Cloud Desktop. Still works offline
Peer to Peer Instant Messaging

Why build such a thing?

We have predicted the future and can see Microsoft and Apple Operating Systems only becoming worse for the users. There will be little to no offline support and users will be forced into paying monthly subscription fees in order to have access to their "Virtual Cloud Computer".


Performing any basic functionality like ordering a pizza or writing a Word document will be a draconian experience. Overall quality of User Experience will continue to decline and humans will suffer from not being able to pay their Microsoft Live Subscriptions.


How can I download my own Buddy Pond?

Buddy Pond works locally first. It's your own Cloud. The entire Desktop experience is ready to go immediately without having to wait.


You can download our Companion App: MY BUDDY right now and everything works offline and stores your files locally.


What is the Buddy Pond AppStore?

The Buddy Pond AppStore is a library of community contributed Apps specifically built, tested, and designed for Buddy Pond. It's still in Alpha and we have over 9000 Apps in testing.


If you'd like to see your App added please message us in the #Alpha Pond.


What are the goals of Buddy Pond?


We would like to be able to turn on our computer and easily communicate with friends and family without having to pay Microsoft or Apple a monthly subscription fee.


We would like able to turn on our gaming system and not be forced to update for three hours on a Sunday morning.


We would like to be able to manage our devices, our businesses, and our home and office networks without being dependant on any Internet or Cloud Providers.


We would like to have all our favorite apps ( Chat / Social / Work / Music Production / Gaming / Art / Visual Effects ) all one place.


We would like to be able to know in twenty years time all of this will still work exactly the same.
