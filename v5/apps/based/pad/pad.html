Frequently Asked Questions

What is required for a Pad?

At a minimum, Pads require an index.html file in the root of the Pad.
This index.html will serve as the entry point for your application.

Can I deploy TypeScript applications? React? Web Assembly? WebGL?

Yes. Anything that runs in the browser will work in a BuddyPond Pad.
Pads are backed by a standard Content Delivery Network which serves all your files as expected.

Where are my files hosted? How do I link to my files?

Your BuddyPond files are located at: https://files.buddypond.com/

Your home directory is your username: https://buddypond.com/

In most cases, use relative links to link assets in your Pad code.

How much storage is available?

Currently each Buddy receives 10mb of free storage. Open the to see your current usage.
