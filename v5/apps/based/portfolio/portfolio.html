Buddy Pond BuddyCoins Ecosystem

Off-chain / In-house Coins

$GBP – Good Buddy Points
The default off-chain currency of Buddy Pond. Earned automatically just for being online — 10 points per minute! Use $GBP to reward friends, cast spells, grow your aura, and more.

$MEGA – Megabytes
A coin tied to your BuddyFiles storage. Each $MEGA equals 1MB of storage. You start with 10 $MEGA and can trade it like any other coin in the system.

$BUX – BuddyBux
A stable coin locked to $1.00 USD. BUX is used to unlock premium Buddy Pond features and services. You can purchase BUX with real money.
